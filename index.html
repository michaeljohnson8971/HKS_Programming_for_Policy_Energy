    The U.S. infrastructure landscape is seeing some of its greatest change, ever. The IRA and IIJA bills redouble the nation’s focus and funding on infrastructure growth – but also shift the balance between infrastructure sectors. As the landscape evolves, construction workers and prospective workers can benefit by keeping apprised of the most in-demand sectors, employers can benefit by gauging workforce trends, and construction job-placement organizations can benefit by better meeting the shifting balance of the labor force
